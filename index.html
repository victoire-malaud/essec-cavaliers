This is the most anticipated event for ESSECavaliers members! This show jumping competition brings together 3 major French universities in Singapore (ESSEC, EDHEC and INSEAD). Above all a great moment of conviviality and sharing around the horse, the Dressage des Grandes Ecoles is the only competition organised by a school to offer 3 different dressage exercises (you know: this sequence of figures that the rider performs with his horse, where they seem to dance together). Competitors from all French schools meet for one day to put dressage in the spotlight, in our prestigious partner stables, the Bukit Timah Saddle Club.
